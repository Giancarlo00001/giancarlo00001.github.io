Most businesses are fully automated within 7-14 days. This includes ad campaign setup, AI training on your business, CRM integration, and calendar connection. We handle everything so you can focus on closing deals.

Yes! We're not just an AI tool - we're a complete done-for-you service. Our team creates, manages, and optimizes your Facebook, Instagram, and Google ad campaigns to ensure you're getting quality leads that our AI can convert into appointments.
