Why do people get final expense insurance?

Final expense insurance is a way to make sure that your family and dependants don't suffer financially when you pass. Final expense insurance can be used to pay for a funeral, pay off debts (including medical bills), pay for legal expenses and help your family maintain their standard of living when they no longer have you to support them.

When do I need final expense insurance?

If you're age 50-85 and want to ensure your family isn't burdened with funeral costs and final expenses, now is the perfect time. Getting coverage while you're healthy can result in lower premiums.

Get Help →

Does my age affect my cost?

Yes, your age can affect your premiums. Your insurance company will evaluate the risk you pose and place you in a 'risk grouping'. Usually, the younger and healthier you are, the lower the premiums that you have to pay.
